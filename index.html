The Problem

Students rarely notice burnout while it is building.

Most tools arrive too late.

Academic pressure, poor sleep, packed schedules, and reduced recovery often build gradually. By the time students realize they are struggling, burnout and declining performance may already be affecting daily life. Burnout Beacon is designed to surface those warning signs earlier.

The Solution

A personal check engine light for student well-being.

Instead of diagnosing conditions or comparing students to generic averages, the platform learns a student's baseline and explains meaningful changes in sleep, workload, and check-ins.

Stable

Your patterns look close to your normal baseline.

Under Pressure

Early warning signs suggest planning and recovery would help.

Strained

Multiple signals are moving in a concerning direction.

Recovery Mode

Support, resources, and rest should be prioritized now.
